🧶 VR Knitting Row Counter

What is a knitting row counter? It’s a simple tool—physical or digital—that helps you keep track of how many rows you’ve knitted. From scarves and sweaters to intricate lace patterns, knowing your row count is essential to maintain consistency, avoid mistakes, and know exactly where you are in your project.

Why put it in VR? With the counter running inside your Quest headset, you can keep knitting while:

  • 🎬 Binge-watching your favorite series
  • 🎧 Listening to podcasts or music
  • 📺 Following VR tutorials without taking off your headset
  • 🏝️ Relaxing in VR environments—no pausing, no interruptions

✨ Features

👆 Big Tap to Count: One large button to increment row count swiftly—keeping your flow uninterrupted.
➕➖ Adjust As Needed: "+" or "−" buttons let you correct missed rows or backtrack easily.
🔄 Quick Reset: Reset the counter to zero in one tap—perfect when starting a new pattern.
💾 Saves Automatically: Your current row count is stored locally—surviving app closures or headset restarts.
🚫🌐 No Ads. No Accounts. No Internet Needed. A distraction-free, privacy-respecting tool—no cloud required.

🎨 Look & Feel

The UI is clean and minimal—think typical row counters but optimized for VR clarity. Large buttons and clear text ensure visibility in your VR display, and soft color contrasts make it easy on the eyes during long knitting sessions.
